Privacy Policy for ContractorAI

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

ContractorAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name
  • Business Data: Project details, estimates, client information, financial records
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full credit card details)
  • Communication Data: Messages, support requests, feedback

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction data, session duration
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (only when you use location-based features like job site tracking)
  • Photos & Camera: Images you capture for receipts and job site documentation
  • Calendar Data: Appointment and scheduling information (only with your permission)

1.3 Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services that may collect data:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Google Calendar: Calendar integration (optional)
  • Google OAuth: Authentication and Gmail integration

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: Provide and maintain ContractorAI features and functionality
  • Business Operations: Process payments, manage your account, store your business data
  • Communication: Send updates, notifications, and respond to inquiries
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and user experience
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other illegal activities
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 We Share Information With:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (hosting), Stripe (payments), AWS (infrastructure)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Protection: To protect rights, property, or safety of ContractorAI, users, or others

3.2 We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing to others
  • Access your data without permission

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access with authentication requirements
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure, enterprise-grade servers
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and monitoring

Data Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable location tracking, notifications, or analytics
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions for camera, calendar, or location access

To exercise these rights, contact us at the information below.

6. Children's Privacy

ContractorAI is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to non-discrimination

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Privacy Shield compliance (where applicable)
  • Adequate data protection measures

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication and security
  • User preferences and settings
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

You can control cookies through your device settings.

10. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Update notice on our website

Continued use of ContractorAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours and provide details about:

  • The nature of the breach
  • Data potentially affected
  • Steps we're taking to address it
  • Actions you should take

App Permissions Explained

ContractorAI requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture receipts and job site photos for your business records
  • Photo Library: To save and access project photos and receipts
  • Location: To track job sites and calculate travel distances for estimates
  • Calendar: To sync appointments and job schedules with your business calendar
  • Notifications: To receive reminders, updates, and important alerts

All permissions are optional and can be managed in your device settings. The app will function with limited features if permissions are denied.
